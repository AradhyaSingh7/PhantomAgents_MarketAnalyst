Here's a summary of the company's financial performance, broken down by key areas:

## Revenue and Profitability##

The company has demonstrated a consistent upward trend in revenue from Q1 2020 to Q4 2024, starting at $16.8 million and reaching $43.8 million by the end of 2024. This growth has been accompanied by an increasing gross profit, from $8.6 million in Q1 2020 to $24.5 million in Q4 2024. The gross margin percentage has also seen a steady increase, moving from 51.2% in Q1 2020 to 55.9% in Q4 2024, indicating improved efficiency in managing the cost of goods sold relative to revenue. However, there was a dip in revenue to $40.2 million and gross profit to $22.5 million in Q1 2025, with the gross margin remaining strong at 56.0%. The first quarter of 2025 also saw a notable decrease in net income to $1.97 million from $4.59 million in the previous quarter.

## Operating Expenses and Income##

Operating expenses have also increased over the period, rising from $9.4 million in Q1 2020 to $19.2 million in Q4 2024. A significant portion of these expenses is allocated to R&D, which grew from $4.2 million to $8.0 million, and selling expenses, which increased from $3.0 million to $6.4 million. Administrative expenses have shown a more moderate increase from $2.2 million to $4.8 million. Despite the rise in operating expenses, the company has transitioned from an operating loss in the early quarters of 2020 to a positive operating income. The operating income peaked at $5.3 million in Q4 2022 before experiencing a decline in Q1 2023 to $3.3 million and subsequently recovering to $5.6 million by Q4 2023. However, the first quarter of 2025 shows a decrease in operating income to $2.7 million.

## Net Income and Earnings Per Share##

The company began 2020 with a net loss of $0.92 million and a basic EPS of -$0.09 in Q1 2020. It achieved profitability by Q4 2020 with a net income of $0.265 million and a basic EPS of $0.02. Profitability has generally increased since then, reaching a high of $4.715 million in net income and $0.42 in EPS in Q4 2024. However, similar to operating income, Q1 2025 saw a significant drop in net income to $1.97 million and EPS to $0.17.

## Cash Flow and Liquidity##

Free cash flow has shown a positive trend, growing from -$0.45 million in Q1 2020 to $2.1 million in Q4 2024. The company's cash and equivalents have also steadily increased, from $4.8 million to $10.4 million over the same period. This indicates a strong ability to generate cash from operations and manage its liquid assets effectively.

## Balance Sheet Highlights##

Total assets have grown consistently from $22.5 million in Q1 2020 to $36.8 million in Q4 2024. This growth is reflected across various asset categories, including accounts receivable, inventory, and property, plant, and equipment. Shareholders' equity has also increased from $13.6 million to $24.0 million, indicating a healthy growth in the company's net worth. Total liabilities have seen a gradual increase from $8.9 million to $12.8 million, suggesting that the asset growth has outpaced the growth in liabilities, leading to a stronger equity position.
