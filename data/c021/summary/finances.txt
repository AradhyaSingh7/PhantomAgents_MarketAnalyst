## Revenue and Profitability##
The company has shown a consistent upward trend in revenue and profitability from Q1 2020 to Q4 2024. Revenue grew from $9 million in Q1 2020 to $45.2 million in Q4 2024. Gross profit also saw a significant increase, rising from $3.8 million to $21 million over the same period. The gross margin percentage has steadily improved, starting at 42.2% in Q1 2020 and reaching 46.5% in Q4 2024, indicating improved efficiency in managing the cost of goods sold.

## Operating Expenses and Income##
Operating expenses have also increased over the years, from $4.8 million in Q1 2020 to $10.8 million in Q4 2024. This increase is driven by rises in Research and Development (R&D), selling, and administrative expenses. Despite the rise in operating expenses, the operating income has shifted from a negative $1 million in Q1 2020 to a positive $10.2 million in Q4 2024, demonstrating strong operational performance. The R&D expenses have grown from $2.2 million to $4.6 million, selling expenses from $1.6 million to $3 million, and administrative expenses from $1 million to $3.2 million.

## Financial Health and Cash Flow##
The company's financial health has also strengthened. Net income has surged from a loss of $1.06 million in Q1 2020 to $9.78 million in Q4 2024. Basic Earnings Per Share (EPS) has improved from -$0.12 to $0.59 in the same period. Free cash flow has also turned positive, growing from -$700,000 in Q1 2020 to $1.7 million in Q4 2024, suggesting the company is generating more cash than it spends on operations and capital expenditures.

## Balance Sheet Overview##
Cash and equivalents have increased from $3 million in Q1 2020 to $4.9 million in Q4 2024. Inventory and accounts receivable have also grown, reflecting increased business activity. Total assets have grown from $14.2 million to $20.5 million, while total liabilities have seen a modest increase from $6.3 million to $7 million. This has resulted in a substantial increase in shareholders' equity, from $7.9 million to $13.5 million, indicating a stronger equity base. Property, plant, and equipment have also increased from $4.6 million to $5.8 million.
